SportPesa aviator challenge with over KSh 10 million in free bets to be won

multibet
•
July 3, 2025

SportPesa Kenya, the country’s leading online gaming platform, has launched its biggest Aviator event yet — the Aviator Challenge. Players now have a chance to earn their share of over KSh 10 million in free bets by completing in-game tasks and climbing the Aviator leaderboard.

The Aviator Challenge is designed to transform how Kenyans engage with the popular crash-style game. It brings together elements of speed, skill, and competition — rewarding players not just for participation, but for precision, persistence, and performance. Whether it’s through fast-paced “Races” or strategy-focused “xTournaments,” this campaign redefines digital gaming with purpose.

What is the Aviator Challenge?

The Aviator Challenge is a live, in-game competition hosted within the SportPesa online casino. Players join either a Race or xTournament, complete tasks, earn points, and move up the leaderboard.

  • Race: Players race to complete specific tasks as quickly as possible. Progress is tracked in real time. Finish first to win big.
  • Aviator xTournament: Players accumulate cashout multipliers — each 1x multiplier equals 1 point. The player with the highest total multiplier score tops the leaderboard.

How to join the Aviator Challenge

  1. Log in to www.ke.sportpesa.com and launch Aviator
  2. Click the banner announcing the current Race or xTournament
  3. Review the task list for that challenge
  4. Click Join to enter
  5. Play Aviator, complete tasks, and track your score on the leaderboard
  6. Climb the ranks and win free bets once the challenge ends

Whether you’re betting KSh 5 or KSh 50,000, every player who meets the minimum stake requirement can compete.

“This is no longer just about betting — it’s about strategy, speed, and rising to the top,” said Willis Ojwang, Head of PR at SportPesa. “The Aviator Challenge brings out Kenya’s most competitive players — and rewards them like never before.”

What kind of tasks will you face?

Tasks vary per event and may include:

  • Cashing out on specific multipliers
  • Winning a set number of rounds
  • Betting a total amount
  • Playing a required number of rounds
  • Collecting multipliers in multiple sessions

Every completed task earns you points — and top scorers win a share of the prize pool.

Over KSh 10 million in free bets up for grabs

  • Leaderboards determine all prize winners
  • Top-ranking players earn the largest free bet amounts
  • Free bets are credited to your SportPesa casino account
  • Prizes can be used to play Aviator and win real cash

Winners are notified in-game after each event and can claim prizes instantly through the Aviator Free Bet.

Why this challenge matters

This is more than just entertainment — it’s an open invitation to compete, win, and be recognized. The Aviator Challenge taps into Kenya’s fast-growing digital gaming culture and offers players daily opportunities to rise to the top — with real rewards.

“You’re not just betting anymore,” Ojwang added. “You’re playing with purpose, earning every point, and pushing for the top spot. This is how champions are made.”

How to start climbing the leaderboard

  • Log in at www.ke.sportpesa.com
  • Open Aviator, click Join, and start the challenge
  • Play smart, complete tasks, and watch your points rise
  • Dominate the leaderboard. Collect your free bets. Win big

As you take flight in the Aviator Challenge, SportPesa also invites you to go even further through the Tujiamini Initiative — a national movement that funds up to KSh 30 million every year for sports, talent, and community projects across Kenya.
