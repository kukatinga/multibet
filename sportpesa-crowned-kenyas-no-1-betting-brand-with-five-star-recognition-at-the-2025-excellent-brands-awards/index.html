In a year defined by record wins, grassroots impact, and unmatched innovation, SportPesa has officially been crowned Kenya’s No.1 betting brand after earning the coveted Five-Star Award at the 2025 Excellent Brands Awards, held at Villa Rosa Kempinski, Nairobi.

The recognition places SportPesa not only at the summit of Kenya’s gaming industry but as a national symbol of trust, innovation, and community empowerment.

More than an award — A testament to impact
SportPesa’s Five-Star recognition is not just a trophy. Five stars, One leader
At the 2025 Excellent Brands Awards, where top players in agriculture, fintech, logistics, hospitality, and entertainment were recognized, SportPesa stood out as the only brand to deliver on performance, consumer trust, and social responsibility at equal measure.
This five-star crown cements SportPesa as the undisputed market leader and best betting brand in Africa, trusted not just for thrilling gaming experiences, but for building futures and fostering national pride.
