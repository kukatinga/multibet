Kenya’s biggest betting win: Bonnie Kamau takes KSh 424 million SportPesa Mega Jackpot

M
multibet
•
August 13, 2025

Kenya’s sports betting scene has reached a historic milestone after Bonnie Kamau secured the country’s largest-ever payout, walking away with an incredible KSh 424,660,618.

Kamau’s victory came in the SportPesa Mega Jackpot, where he correctly predicted all 17 match outcomes on August 3, 2025. This is the first time the jackpot has been claimed since 2018, ending a seven-year drought and creating a wave of excitement among punters nationwide.

From a KSh 99 stake to millions

The SportPesa Mega Jackpot is Kenya’s most iconic football betting challenge, offering millions in prizes for predicting the results of 17 carefully selected weekend fixtures. Kamau’s journey began with a single KSh 99 bet, a deep knowledge of football, and the courage to trust his instincts — turning a modest stake into the largest jackpot prize in the nation’s history.

His win, crowned as the biggest betting win in Kenya, together with other landmark victories, has cemented SportPesa’s reputation as the home of record-breaking payouts. Each winner’s story proves that with the right predictions and a small stake, life-changing fortunes are within reach. With the jackpot now reset, the stage is set for the next name to be added to this elite list of champions.

Past Winners’ hall of fame:

  • 2017 – Samuel Abisai – KSh 221,301,602
  • 2018 – Gordon Ogada – KSh 230,742,881 (previous record)
  • 2018 – Cosmas Korir – KSh 208,733,619
  • 2025 – Bonnie Kamau – KSh 424,660,618 (current record)

Why the Mega Jackpot stands out

The Mega Jackpot is more than just a betting pool — it’s a cultural event capable of turning ordinary Kenyans into multimillionaires.

Key features include:

  • Low Entry Cost: Only KSh 99 per bet.
  • Multiple Categories: 13/13, 14/14, 15/15, 16/16, and 17/17 games.
  • Progressive Growth: Prize pool increases weekly if there’s no winner.
  • Bonus Payouts: Rewards even for near wins (12–16 correct predictions in the 17/17 category).

Bonnie’s record-breaking win is more than a moment in history — it is living proof that the SportPesa Mega Jackpot delivers real, life-changing results. Following his triumph, the jackpot has been reset to KSh 100 million, sparking nationwide excitement. Each new cycle presents a fresh opportunity for someone to transform a modest KSh 99 stake into millionaire status, keeping the dream alive for thousands of hopeful punters.

How to play and win – Your turn to become a legendary winner

The mega jackpot has now reset to KSh 100 million, and SportPesa is inviting players to take their shot at glory.

Getting started is simple:

  • Via SMS: –
    • Pick your jackpot category (13–17 games).
    • SMS your predictions to 79079 (use “0” for skipped games).
    • Stake is KSh 99 per bet.
  • Via Web: –
    • Log into your SportPesa account.
    • Select Home win, Draw, or Away win for each match.
    • Confirm your bet.

Winning tips to guide you when staking

  • Follow team form and injury updates.
  • Study past jackpot results to spot patterns.
  • Balance favourites with potential upsets.

The impact of Kenya’s biggest betting win

This biggest betting win in Kenya has not only transformed Kamau’s life but has also positioned Kenya as a major player in Africa’s sports betting industry. Analysts predict increased participation and even bigger payouts in future cycles as more players are inspired to take their shot. With the jackpot now at KSh 100 million, competition for the next win is already heating up.
