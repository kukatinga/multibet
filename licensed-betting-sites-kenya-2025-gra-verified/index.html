The Shocking Reality: Why License Numbers Matter

You might think: “But that new betting app has better odds and a 500% bonus!”

Here’s what those license numbers actually mean for YOUR safety:

  • Player Protection: Licensed operators must keep player funds in separate accounts. Your deposits aren’t mixed with their operating money.

  • Dispute Resolution: Got a problem? The GRA can intervene. With unlicensed sites, you’re on your own.

  • Fair Gaming: Licensed sites use certified Random Number Generators (RNGs). No rigged games.

  • Responsible Gambling: They must offer deposit limits, self-exclusion, and other protective tools.

True Story: Last month, a punter won Ksh 250,000 on a new, unlicensed site. They asked for “verification documents” 5 times, then disappeared. That Ksh 250,000? Gone forever.
