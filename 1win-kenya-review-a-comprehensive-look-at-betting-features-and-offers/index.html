Bet Types in Sports

Sports betting offers a variety of bet types that cater to different strategies and preferences. Understanding these options can help you make more informed decisions and enhance your overall betting experience. Here are some of the common 1win bet types you can explore on 1win:

  • Moneyline Bets: This straightforward bet involves picking the winner of a match. Each team or player is assigned odds, and you win if your chosen team or player wins the event.
  • Point Spread Bets: In this type of betting, a handicap is given to level the playing field. You can bet on a team to win by a margin or cover the spread.
  • Over/Under Bets: Also known as totals, this bet involves predicting whether the total score or number of points/goals in a game will be over or under a specified number.
  • Parlay Bets: This bet combines multiple individual bets into one, offering a higher payout if all selected outcomes occur. However, if one bet loses, the entire parlay is forfeited.
  • Props Bets: Short for proposition bets, these focus on specific events or outcomes within a game, such as which player will score first or the number of goals scored by a team.
  • Futures Bets: These are bets placed on events that will happen in the future, such as who will win a league or tournament. They often offer good payouts and can be very speculative.
